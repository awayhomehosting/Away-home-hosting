Questions people usually ask

Here are the basics. We’ll walk through the details for your home during the free property check.

How much can my home earn while I’m away?
It depends on your location, size and dates. During your free check we’ll give you a rough estimate based on similar properties and realistic occupancy – not inflated “best case” numbers.
Is there a set-up cost?
There’s no fee just to chat or get an estimate. If your home is suitable and you decide to list, we work on a percentage of each booking. You only pay when guests actually stay.
Who is this best suited for?
Homeowners or long-term renters in Melbourne who are away for stretches of time – holidays, work trips or staying elsewhere – and want their place to earn instead of sitting empty.
What happens to my belongings?
We’ll help you decide what stays, what’s locked away and what should be stored. Certain cupboards or rooms can remain locked and off-limits to guests.
Can I choose the dates it’s available?
Absolutely. You control the calendar. We only open the dates you’re comfortable with, and you can always block out dates you want your home back.
Where do you operate?
We’re based in Melbourne and currently focus on inner-city and nearby suburbs. If you’re not sure whether your place is in range, just ask during the enquiry.
